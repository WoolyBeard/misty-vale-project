## Special Abilities

- **Heads:** 1d8+4 heads; 1HD per head.
- **Disabling heads:** For every 8hp damage taken, one head is disabled (cannot attack).
- **Variants:** Special hydras are sometimes found with venom, fiery breath, etc.
