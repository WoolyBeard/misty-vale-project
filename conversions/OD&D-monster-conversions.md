# OD&D to Dragonbane Conversion

## Armor Value

Armor Value = **9 - AC**

| OD&D AC | Armor | Dragonbane Armor Value |
|---------:|-------|-----------------------:|
| AC 8 | Leather | 1 |
| AC 7 | Studded | 2 |
| AC 5 | Chain | 4 |
| AC 3 | Plate | 6 |
| etc. | | |

---

## Hit Points

### Under 2 HD

Use **10 HP base**, +/- 1 = +/- 2.

| Hit Dice | HP |
|----------:|---:|
| HD ½ | 5 HP |
| HD 1-1 | 8 HP |
| HD 1 | 10 HP |
| HD 1+1 | 12 HP |

### 2 HD+

Use **7 HP per HD** plus any static bonus.

| Hit Dice | HP |
|----------:|---:|
| HD 2 | 14 HP |
| HD 2+2 | 18 HP |
| HD 3 | 20 HP |
| HD 3+1 | 21 HP |
| HD 4+1 | 30 HP |
| etc. | |

---

## Damage

Damage is usually about double, likewise, but single target damage should not exceed **4 dice**.
