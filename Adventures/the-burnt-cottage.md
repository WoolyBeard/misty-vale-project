# Burnt Out Cottage of the mad fire mage 

*A small adventure for Dragonbane*

## Background

Years ago, a reclusive mage settled in this lonely farmhouse to continue his magical studies far from civilization.

Obsessed with the secrets of fire, he attempted a dangerous summoning ritual that quickly spiraled out of control. The spell tore open reality for only a heartbeat but it was long enough.

Something answered.

The farmhouse erupted into flames. By the time anyone arrived, the building was a smoldering ruin. The mage was never seen again, and locals have avoided the place ever since.

The creature still remains.

---

## Adventure Hooks

The characters discover the burnt farmhouse while traveling, searching for shelter, or following rumors of an abandoned wizard's home.
The party see a smoldering bit of smoke on the horizon.
While staying in outskirts someone mentions the old cinder mage from up north hasn't been in to get his provisions of bat guano and saltpeter. 
---

# Area 1 - Burnt Farmhouse

The farmhouse is little more than blackened stone walls surrounding the charred remains of a collapsed upper floor. Rain and time have reclaimed much of the structure.

The only intact routes underground are:

- A weathered cellar door firmly shut outside.
- A jagged hole in the burned floor where the collapse exposed part of the cellar below.

### What the Characters Notice

Before entering the cellar:

- Faint scratching echoes from beneath the floorboards.
- Occasionally something lets out a dry simmering blast of hot air.
- Dust or smoke? drifts upward through the hole as something shifts below.
- Sunlight streaming through the collapsed roof falls directly onto a single parchment resting on the cellar floor.

The parchment is clearly visible, but anything moving below remains hidden in the darkness with the occasional fiery glow just out of eyesight. 

---

# Area 2 - Cellar

The cellar mostly escaped the fire.

Shelves have collapsed into heaps of rotten wood and broken bottles. The smell of soot has long since faded, replaced by damp earth and mold.

Near the center of the room lies the mage's skeleton, little more than scattered bones wrapped in burned robes.

Beside the body rests a loose page from his grimoire.

The cellar is home to a **Calydon**, summoned during the failed ritual some time ago. It lurks in the shadows and attacks anyone entering its lair.

---

# The Grimoire Page

The surviving page contains fragments of the mage's research.

The page is valuable to scholars and mages, even though it is incomplete. 

---

# Conclusion

If the Calydon is slain, the farmhouse finally falls silent.

Nothing else of value remains. Time and fire claimed everything except the lone page of the mage's work.
